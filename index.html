Who We Are

About Us

The Japan Desk is an initiative born out of a vision to bridge the gap between India and Japan in the realms of academia and industry. By providing comprehensive support and resources, we aim to enhance human resource development, foster groundbreaking research, and stimulate economic growth through collaborative efforts. Our goal is to build a community where ideas and expertise flow freely, leading to transformative impacts on both societies.

Welcome to Japan Desk at IIT Hyderabad

Discover a hub where innovation meets collaboration. At Japan Desk, we strive to create a dynamic platform fostering strong academic and industrial partnerships between India and Japan.

Our Mission

Bridging through Innovation and Collaboration" Japan Desk aims to enhance academic and industrial synergy by facilitating seamless interactions and joint ventures. Our mission is to promote mutual growth and understanding through innovative collaborations and cultural exchanges.

Message from the Head of Japan Desk

As a part of the active collaboration between Japan and IIT Hyderabad, 'Japan Desk', is launched at IIT Hyderabad and this is the first-of-its-kind initiative among all IITs to facilitate India - Japan Academic & Industrial collaborations. We aim to provide a one-stop solution for all the student, faculty and company queries facilitated through this portal and unlock the full potential of our collaboration for mutual benefit. We anticipate enhanced Human Resources development in the fields of academia and industry through our strong collaboration.
